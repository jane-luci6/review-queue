Customer email journey

Subject lines & preheader text

Enter the subject line and preheader text manually each time you create a new campaign in ActiveCampaign. Personalization tags: %FIRSTNAME% and %PROPERTY%. Email 6 uses #PropertyName# in the subject line — confirm the correct AC tag before sending.

  • Email 1 · Welcome

    Subject This is your official welcome to the LUCI family!
    Preheader Here's what happens next, and what we need from you before your kick-off call.

  • Email 2 · Pre-kickoff

    Subject A few reminders before your LUCI kickoff call
    Preheader LUCI is in motion, and here's what we need from you.

  • Email 3 · Install confirmed

    Subject Your LUCI install date is confirmed — here's what to prepare
    Preheader A few things to have ready before the team arrives on %INSTALLATION_DATE%.

  • Email 4 · Pre-install

    Subject We are one week from your LUCI install — let's confirm a few details
    Preheader Three quick items to have in place before %INSTALLATION_DATE%.

  • Email 5 · Go live

    Subject LUCI is live! Check out these resources and support access
    Preheader Everything you need to get the most out of your system, in one place.

  • Email 6 · Survey

    Subject How did we do at #PropertyName#?
    Preheader Takes two minutes to share your experience — we're listening.

  • Email 7 · Signal welcome

    Subject Welcome to The Signal, LUCI's customer newsletter
    Preheader A monthly look at what's new in LUCI, straight from the engineers who build it.

  • Email 8 · 30-day check-in

    Subject You're one month in with LUCI — how is everything running?
    Preheader Your FAQ, feature request process, and support portal.

  • Email 9 · 60-day check-in

    Subject Your LUCI Field Activation Guide is ready
    Preheader A practical look at what each team can do with the platform.

  • Email 10 · 90-day check-in

    Subject How are we going to elevate your guest experience next year?
    Preheader A few things worth putting on your radar as you head into budget season.
