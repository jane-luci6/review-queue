Beyond the platform

What you may not know about LUCI

Most clients come to LUCI for the platform. What many don't know is that the same FDEs who deployed your system stay with you, working as an extension of your team.

Our founders spent fifty years as integrators, operators, and owners. Here's what that looks like in practice:

Technology consulting
Weighing a new system or an expansion? Email your FDE, and we'll get on the calendar. We've sat on your side of the table and catch the questions that usually get skipped before budget is signed.
Design & owner's rep
Building or changing a space? Send us the plans. We join your architect, scope AV and multimedia for how you operate, and hold the standard. You can bid install elsewhere if you prefer; we'll align the result either way.
Budget planning
When we design a space, we build the budget from your plans as part of the package, so you have real numbers before bid and a clear view of where to trim without hurting operations.
Sister properties
Another site not on LUCI yet? Onboarding is shorter than most expect, with the same team and the same standards. Let's talk when you're ready.
