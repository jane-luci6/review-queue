## Welcome body
Every complex environment is, in some sense, a prediction. The infrastructure laid today is a guess about what the property will need to do five years from now. The systems installed this quarter are a bet on the questions that will be asked next quarter, and the one after that.

The work is to make the prediction deliberately. To build now for a version of tomorrow that hasn't fully arrived — a property running on infrastructure it doesn't yet need, supported by a team it hasn't yet had to call, capable of things it hasn't yet been asked to do.
