In the field

Three days to a future-ready platform

Ameristar Council Bluffs Casino Council Bluffs, Iowa · Penn Entertainment

The challenge

Ameristar Council Bluffs spans a land-based casino and a riverboat. A major land-side buildout and a planned transition off the riverboat were approaching, but a decade of aging, patched-together equipment had the team working around its own systems instead of running them. Before the property could make deliberate decisions about its next phase, it needed to clear out the old infrastructure and stand on a unified, future-ready platform.

What LUCI did

LUCI cleared the deck in under three days. The team relocated all AV processing from the riverboat into the property's IT data center on land, eliminating dedicated AV rooms from the new buildout and reducing future construction costs. We replaced the aging head-end systems, amplifiers, and control infrastructure, modernized the system documentation, and brought everything onto LUCI, all without disrupting daily operations.

The results

With the old systems cleared and LUCI in place, Ameristar manages all of its complex A/V from a single interface, giving every member of the team one place to control the property from anywhere. Instead of reacting to aging equipment, the team can now make proactive, deliberate decisions about the guest experience. That foundation supports unlimited operational users on any browser-based device, built-in automation for daily resets and scheduled adjustments, and ongoing enhancements without additional upgrade licensing costs. In practice, that means:

  • One unified view of every endpoint
  • Broader delegation by zone
  • Faster response to daily needs
  • Automated resets and scheduling
  • Simpler maintenance and expansion
  • Lower long-term infrastructure costs

With the system centralized on land, the property is also set up for what comes next: a clean break from the riverboat when the time comes, and a new buildout that plugs into the platform already powering the property; no second install, no parallel system to stand up.

Inside LUCI

Set a morning reset

One of the most useful things on the platform takes about ten minutes to set up, then runs every day without you.

Here's the idea: build a preset that puts your entire property into a known, intentional state, then schedule it to fire before the floor opens.

What it sets:

  • Right channels on the right screens
  • Volumes calibrated for the time of day
  • Each venue configured the way you designed it

What you get: a property that starts each day the way you planned, not the way it ended up after the last shift. No pit boss finding Judge Judy on the sportsbook screens. No morning walk-through to fix volumes that got knocked around overnight.

To set one up:

  1. Go to Presets and create a new preset with the state you want

  2. Drop it onto the Calendar

  3. Set it to repeat daily at your preferred time

If you'd like help building it out, reach out to your FDE. It's a short conversation and one of the highest-ROI calls you'll make.
