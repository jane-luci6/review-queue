USAGE RULES
Default to the full lockup. The mark and wordmark together are the canonical signature. Use mark-only only when space is severely constrained (favicon, social avatar, section accent).
Pair color with background. Black on light, white on dark, mint as an accent on dark. Never use the mint variant on a light background — contrast is too low.
Clear space. Maintain a minimum padding equal to the mark's height on all sides. Never crop or overlap the logo with photography or other elements.
Don't recolor. Use the supplied color variants only. Don't apply gradients, drop shadows, outlines, or rotation. Don't separate the mark from the wordmark to create new lockups.
